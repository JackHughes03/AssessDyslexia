About Us

Dyslexia Assessments is dedicated to enhancing educational outcomes for students with dyslexia.

We complete full cognitive assessments identifying strengths and weaknesses.

We offer thorough assessments, consultations, and support for families, teachers and SENCos.

We believe in empowering educators and families with the knowledge to support learning effectively.

Our Commitment to Educational Excellence

At Dyslexia Assessments, we provide expert evaluations and advice on special educational needs.

Our team specialises in SENCo services, helping educators to support students effectively.

We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to succeed.
